Brum Tech Scene

Interviews and conversations with interesting people doing interesting things in the Birmingham tech community

Susannah Goh

Mon 13 October 2014
By Stuart Langridge

The Brum Tech Scene Interviews are a series of video conversations with interesting and enlightening people involved with technology in some way in Birmingham. This discussion is with Susannah Goh, Innovative Health and Digital Media lead at Birmingham Science City, chair of the Open Data Forum, and skateboarder.

If you have thoughts, suggestions, ideas for people to talk to, bouquets, brickbats, or just want a chat, discuss these interviews on the birmingham.io forum.

This is an sil thing.
